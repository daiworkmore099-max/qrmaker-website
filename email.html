Email QR Code FAQs
Which email apps does it open?
The mailto: link opens the default email app on the phone — Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail, or any other default app the user has set.
Can I add CC or BCC?
Not directly in the form — but you can use the plain text QR and manually write: mailto:email@x.com?subject=X&cc=other@x.com
