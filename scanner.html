QR Scanner FAQs
Does it work on mobile browsers?
Yes. Works on Chrome, Safari, Firefox on Android and iPhone. Camera permission required. For iPhone, use Safari for best experience.
Can I scan a screenshot of a QR code?
Yes. Use the "Upload Image" tab to upload a screenshot, photo, or any image containing a QR code. It scans and decodes automatically.
Is my camera feed sent to any server?
No. Camera scanning happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No video or images are ever uploaded or sent to any server.
Why isn't it detecting the QR code?
Ensure good lighting, hold the camera steady, and make sure the full QR code is visible in the frame. For dark or damaged QR codes, try the image upload method with a high-contrast photo.
