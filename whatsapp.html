WhatsApp QR Code FAQs
Does the WhatsApp QR code work for +91 Indian numbers?
Yes. Enter your 10-digit mobile number and select +91 India. The QR code generates a wa.me link with your full number including country code. Works with any Indian mobile number.
Does the person need to save my number first?
No. When they scan the QR code, WhatsApp opens directly to chat with you — no need to save the number first. This is the main advantage of WhatsApp QR codes for businesses.
Will it work on both Android and iPhone?
Yes. The wa.me link format works on WhatsApp for both Android and iOS. The phone camera or any QR scanner app can scan it.
Can I add a pre-filled message?
Yes. Enter any message in the "Pre-filled Message" field. When the customer scans and opens WhatsApp, they'll see this message in the text box — they just need to press Send.
Is there a difference between WhatsApp and WhatsApp Business?
No difference in the QR code format. The same wa.me link works for both regular WhatsApp and WhatsApp Business accounts. Use whichever you have.
