Location QR Code FAQs
Which map app does it open?
For Google Maps URLs, it opens Google Maps. For geo: coordinates, Android opens Google Maps by default. iPhone may open Apple Maps — use Google Maps URL for consistent experience.
How accurate is the GPS coordinates option?
GPS coordinates are exact — they pinpoint a specific location on earth. The "Use My Location" button uses your device's GPS for high accuracy.
Can I use an Ola Maps or Apple Maps link?
You can paste any URL as a location QR — it works like a URL QR code. For widest compatibility, Google Maps links are recommended.
