How to Make Bulk QR Codes from Excel / Google Sheets

Export your Excel or Google Sheets data as a CSV file (File → Download → CSV). The first column should be the label (name), and the second column the content (URL, UPI ID, phone number, etc.). Upload the CSV to QRMaker's bulk generator and all QR codes are created instantly.

Bulk QR Code FAQs
How many QR codes can I generate at once?
QRMaker supports up to 50 QR codes per batch. For larger batches, split your CSV into groups of 50 and generate multiple times. All downloads are free with no watermark.
What CSV format should I use?
For URL batch: column 1 = label (name), column 2 = URL. For UPI batch: column 1 = name, column 2 = UPI ID, column 3 = amount (optional). You can also simply paste one item per line in the text tab without needing a CSV.
Can I download all QR codes as a single ZIP file?
Yes. After generating, click "Download All (ZIP)" to get all QR codes in a single ZIP file. Each QR code is saved as a PNG file labeled with its name from column 1 of your CSV.
Is there any watermark on bulk QR codes?
No. All QR codes generated by QRMaker — including bulk — are completely free with no watermark, no logo, and no expiry. They work forever once generated.
Can I change the color and size of bulk QR codes?
Yes. You can set the foreground color, QR size, dot style, and error correction level before generating. The same design settings apply to all QR codes in the batch.
