Digital Visiting Card Kaise Banaye

Printed visiting cards ki zaroorat nahi. QRMaker pe apna naam, phone, email, aur company details bharein. Ek QR code ban jaata hai jo aap apne phone ke back pe ya laptop pe stick kar sakte hain. Koi bhi scan kare aur contact save ho jaaye.

VCard 3.0 Standard

QRMaker uses the vCard 3.0 standard, which is supported by all major operating systems — Android, iOS, Windows, and macOS. The saved contact includes all fields you fill in.

vCard QR Code FAQs
Which phones support vCard QR codes?
All modern smartphones — Android and iPhone. When scanned, the phone shows a "Save Contact" prompt. Works with native camera apps on both platforms.
Can I add multiple phone numbers?
The current form supports one phone number. For multiple numbers, use the plain text QR type and write the vCard manually, or generate one QR per number.
Will the LinkedIn link be saved in contacts?
LinkedIn URL is saved as a URL field in the contact. Most phones display it in the contact card and can open it directly.
