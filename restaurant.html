Restaurant Menu QR Code — Free for Indian Restaurants

Create a digital menu QR code for your restaurant, dhaba, café, or food stall in under a minute. No technical knowledge needed — if you have a menu photo, PDF, or Zomato link, you can create a QR code right now.

Restaurant Menu QR Code Kaise Banaye

Agar aapka menu Zomato ya Swiggy pe hai, toh woh link copy karo aur yahan paste karo — QR code ready. Agar sirf PDF hai, toh Google Drive pe upload karo, shareable link lo, aur yahan paste karo. Agar kuch bhi nahi hai, WhatsApp option use karo — customers WhatsApp karenge menu maangne ke liye.

Print Tips for Restaurant QR Codes

  • Minimum size: 5cm × 5cm for table display
  • Laminate the printed QR code to protect from spills
  • Test before printing in bulk — scan with multiple phones
  • High contrast — black QR on white background scans best
  • Download SVG for best print quality at any size

Benefits of Digital Menu QR Code

  • Update menu prices without reprinting
  • No printing cost for menu changes
  • Customers can view menu before sitting
  • Hygienic — no physical menu passing between customers
  • Works in dim restaurant lighting (phone screen is backlit)
Restaurant Menu QR Code FAQs
Do I need a website for a menu QR code?
No. You can upload your menu PDF to Google Drive and use that link. Or use the WhatsApp option — customers scan and WhatsApp you for the menu. No website needed.
Can I update the menu later?
If you're using a URL (website, Google Drive), update the content at the link — the QR code stays the same. The QR code always opens the same URL, so updating the menu at that URL is enough.
How do I print the table card?
Generate the QR code, click "Print Table Card" to see a printable design with your restaurant name, QR code, and scan instructions. Click "Print This Card" to print directly.
Can I use my Zomato or Swiggy menu link?
Yes. Copy your Zomato or Swiggy restaurant page URL and paste it in the Menu URL field. Customers will be taken directly to your online menu when they scan.
