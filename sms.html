SMS QR Code FAQs
Does SMS QR work on all phones?
Yes. Scanning opens the native SMS app on both Android and iPhone. The phone number and message are pre-filled.
Can the message be longer than 160 characters?
Yes, but long messages create larger QR codes that may be harder to scan. Keep messages under 100 characters for best results.

SMS QR Code for Business — India

SMS QR codes are popular with small businesses in India for customer feedback, support, and order confirmations. Print on packaging, bills, or shop banners so customers can text you instantly. Works on every mobile phone — no internet or app needed for the customer.

Difference Between SMS QR and WhatsApp QR

SMS QR opens the native SMS app and works even without internet or a smartphone with WhatsApp. WhatsApp QR requires the WhatsApp app to be installed. For reaching all customers including those without smartphones, SMS QR is more reliable.

Does the receiver pay for the SMS?
No. The person scanning sends the SMS from their own phone using their carrier plan. Standard SMS charges apply on their end. The receiver gets a normal SMS at no cost.
Can I pre-fill just the phone number without a message?
Yes. Leave the message field blank — the QR will open the SMS app with only the phone number pre-filled. The user can type any message they want before sending.
Does SMS QR work on feature phones (non-smartphones)?
Feature phones generally cannot scan QR codes unless they have a dedicated QR scanner app. On modern smartphones (Android and iPhone), SMS QR codes work natively with the camera app.
